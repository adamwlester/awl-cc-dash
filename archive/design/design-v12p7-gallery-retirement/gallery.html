A single/multi agent picker whose trigger shows the current selection as identity badges. The feed From/To filter, Prompt To, and the From selectors are in-flow accordion drawers (.dd--acc + .src-acc): the drawer pushes the content below down instead of overlaying it, the toggle is sticky (no outside-click dismiss; Esc closes), and the trigger chevron follows the accordion norm (right → down). Run Striprun-strip

A textless run-state bar (opposite polarity, no danger ramp for the live states). The single-colour fill + the barber-pole indeterminate are the floor; the segmented variant renders an agent's self-reported checklist as done÷total segments (navy separators).
